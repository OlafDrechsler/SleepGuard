# Privacy Policy for SleepGuard

_Last updated: 2026-06-21_

SleepGuard is designed to respect your privacy completely.

## No data collection

SleepGuard does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.
The app has **no internet permission** and is technically incapable of sending
or receiving any data over a network.

- No analytics, no tracking, no advertising.
- No user accounts, no cloud storage.
- No data leaves your device.

## Settings stored on your device

The app saves only your own preferences (interval, timeout, button position and
language) locally on your device using Android's standard storage. This data
never leaves the device and is removed when you uninstall the app.

## Permissions

SleepGuard requests only the permissions it needs to function, and uses them
solely for the stated purpose:

- **Display over other apps** – to show the check button on top of other apps.
- **Device administrator (screen lock only)** – to lock the screen, exactly like
  pressing the power button. SleepGuard requests no other device-admin power.
- **Foreground service / exact alarm** – to make the check button appear on time.
- **Ignore battery optimization (optional)** – so the timer keeps working
  reliably while another app is in the foreground.

To stop video playback when you fall asleep, SleepGuard sends a standard "pause"
media-button event (the same as a headset pause button). This requires no special
permission and does not access any personal data.

None of these permissions are used to access, collect, or transmit personal
information.

## Donations

If you choose to support development, the in-app "Support development" button
opens an external PayPal link in your browser. Any payment is handled entirely
by PayPal under PayPal's own privacy policy. SleepGuard itself receives no
payment information.
